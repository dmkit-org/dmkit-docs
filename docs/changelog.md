# What's New

## v1.0.0

Initial release of DMKit.

- Campaign management — create and manage multiple campaigns
- Worlds & Locations — spatial canvases for placing and connecting locations
- Entity library — Characters, Items, and Hazards scoped to a campaign
- Factions, Quests, and Session Notes
- Player Characters
- Battle map — grid-based encounter runner with tokens, HP tracking, and conditions
- AI world map generation
- Campaign export and import
- Google sign-in support
