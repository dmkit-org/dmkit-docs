# Campaigns

## Creating a Campaign

From the Campaigns screen, click **New Campaign**, enter a name and optional description, and click **Create**. Campaign names must be unique per account.

## Managing Campaigns

- **Rename** — click the campaign name inline to edit it
- **Delete** — open the campaign menu and select **Delete**. You will be asked to confirm. Deleting a campaign removes all its data permanently.

## Campaign Privacy

Campaigns are private to your account. No other user can see or access your campaigns.

## Factions, Quests & Session Notes

These campaign-level entities are accessible from the sidebar when you are inside a campaign.

**Factions** group Characters and represent guilds, cults, governments, or any organisation. Each faction has a disposition toward the party.

**Quests** track story objectives. A quest has a status (active, completed, failed, or inactive), a list of objectives, and can reference Characters, Locations, and Items.

**Session Notes** are a chronological log of your play sessions. Each note has a date played, a rich text summary, and can reference Characters and Locations that featured in the session.

## Player Characters

Player Characters (PCs) are managed at the Campaign level since they travel across Worlds. Add your party members under **Player Characters** in the sidebar.
