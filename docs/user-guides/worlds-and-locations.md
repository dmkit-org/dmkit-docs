# Worlds & Locations

## Worlds

A Campaign can have multiple Worlds — a continent, a plane of existence, a city, a dungeon complex. Worlds are spatial canvases where you place and connect Locations.

**Creating a World:**
1. From inside a campaign, click **New World** in the sidebar
2. Give it a name and click **Create**
3. The World canvas opens — blank by default

**Adding a background map:**
- Click **Upload Map** to upload a PNG, JPG, or WebP image as the canvas background
- Click **Generate Map** to describe your world in a text prompt and generate a map image via AI

## Locations

Locations are places within a World — cities, dungeons, forests, temples. Place them on the canvas and connect them with travel routes.

**Adding a Location:**
1. Click **Add Location** on the World canvas
2. A new card appears — click it to rename it
3. Drag it to the position on the canvas where it belongs

**Connecting Locations:**
- Hover over a Location card to reveal its handles
- Drag from a handle to another Location card to create a travel route
- Click the route to add a label (e.g. "5 days by road")

**Drilling in:**
- Double-click a Location card to enter it and see its Areas canvas

## Areas

Areas are spaces within a Location — rooms, clearings, districts, ship decks. They work the same as Locations: place them on the canvas, name them, and double-click to enter.

Double-clicking an Area opens its **battle map**.
